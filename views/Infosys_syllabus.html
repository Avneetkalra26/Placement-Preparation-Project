Infosys Syllabus

The Infosys paper is a written test that is administered to candidates as part of the company's recruitment process. The test is designed to assess the candidate's English language skills, quantitative aptitude, logical reasoning skills, and pseudocode writing ability.

The paper is divided into four sections:

  • Verbal Ability:- This section tests the candidate's ability to read and understand English text. It includes questions on reading comprehension, vocabulary, grammar, and sentence correction.
  • Quantitative Ability:- This section tests the candidate's basic mathematical skills. It includes questions on arithmetic, algebra, geometry, and trigonometry.
  • Logical Reasoning:- This section tests the candidate's basic mathematical skills. It includes questions on arithmetic, algebra, geometry, and trigonometry.
  • PseudocodeThis section tests the candidate's ability to write pseudocode for simple problems. Pseudocode is a plain English description of how to solve a problem, step-by-step.

Tips for preparing for the TCS NQT exam:

  • Thoroughly study the entire Infosys syllabus.
  • Solve previous year papers and sample papers to get an idea of the type of questions asked in the exam.
  • Practice regularly to improve your speed and accuracy.
  • Focus on your weak areas and try to improve them.
  • Get enough sleep and eat a healthy meal on the day of the exam.
