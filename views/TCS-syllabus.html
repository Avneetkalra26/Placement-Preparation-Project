TCS Syllabus

TCS NQT has updated its TCS NQT Syllabus 2023 batches. They have introduced a new curriculum for this year hiring. Some new sections have been added in their selection process. TCS NQT Syllabus for 2023 batch is explained in detail in this article, make sure you go through the entire syllabus, so that you don't miss any point

IMPORTANT NOTE:

  • There will be no negative marking.
  • TCS NQT is Non-adaptive this year
  • You will not get any extra rough paper in the exam as a calculator and Rough Paper will be available on your Desktop Screen. You are not allowed to move your eyes down while giving the examination.

Tips for preparing for the TCS NQT exam:

  • Thoroughly study the entire TCS NQT syllabus.
  • Solve previous year papers and sample papers to get an idea of the type of questions asked in the exam.
  • Practice regularly to improve your speed and accuracy.
  • Focus on your weak areas and try to improve them.
  • Get enough sleep and eat a healthy meal on the day of the exam.
